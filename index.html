Who are ya? CS2

A web-based game inspired on playfootball.games's famous Who Are Ya? (https://playfootball.games/who-are-ya), which gained popularity online in the recent years, but instead of it being football themed, it's based on Counter-Strike! The game works the same way the original game works, using the same concept. You get a random player from the main teams and leagues from all around the world, and the user has to guess who the randomized selected player is. After every guess, the game will present 'feedback' to the user, showing if he got the player correct in a series of different criteria (Region, role, age, rating, major appearances, team, continent, etc). Based on the feedback given to the user, it is now in the user's hands to fix what was wrong in his last guess until he gets all the criteria correct and correctly guesses the player.
